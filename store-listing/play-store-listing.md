Copy-paste source for the Google Play Console store listing. Revised 2026-07-05.

Notes:
- Play Console fields are plain text boxes. Markdown does NOT render there — the
  blocks below are paste-ready plain text.

## App name (30 char max)

Focamai

## Short description (80 char max — currently 79)

Search, answer one question, get 6 honest picks. In and out — no rabbit holes.

## Full description (4000 char max)

Most of us don't actually want to go shopping online. We want a charger, a stroller, a set of batteries, a pair of headphones — whatever it is we happen to need. The problem is that getting that item usually means entering an environment designed to keep us there: trending products, limited-time deals, sponsored results, and pages of near-identical options built to keep us comparing.

Focamai starts from a different place: your needs, not a marketplace's landing page.

HOW IT WORKS

1. Describe what you're looking for, the way you'd tell a salesperson. Plain words are fine.
2. Focamai asks one follow-up question so it understands what actually matters to you — budget, size, who it's for, what you'll use it with.
3. You get six focused picks. Not six hundred results. Six.

HONEST PICKS, NOT SALES PITCHES

Every pick comes with a plain explanation of why it was chosen for your situation — and at least one honest drawback. Maybe it runs over your budget. Maybe it's heavier than the alternatives. Maybe it's the right choice only if you care more about battery life than weight. Focamai doesn't tell you what to buy; it gives you what you need to decide quickly and move on.

IN AND OUT

There's no feed to browse, no recommendations pulling you sideways, no "customers also bought." When a pick looks right, one tap takes you straight to its Amazon product page (US or Canada — your choice). You get the convenience of a large marketplace without wandering through it.

A FEW PRACTICAL EXTRAS

• Refine in your own words — add notes like "must fit in a carry-on" or "quiet enough for a baby's room" and get updated picks.
• Not quite right? Tell Focamai what felt off and it suggests a sharper search.
• Revisit completed searches saved on your device, or run one again when you need a fresh look.

THE REAL TEST

Don't just browse around to see what Focamai looks like. The next time you actually need something, search for it here the way you normally would — and see whether you get from "I need something" to "found it" faster and with less distraction. That's where the value shows up.

Focamai is an independent app and is not affiliated with Amazon. As an Amazon Associate, Focamai earns from qualifying purchases.
