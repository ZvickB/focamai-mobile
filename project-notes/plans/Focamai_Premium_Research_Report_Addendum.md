# Addendum --- Premium Research Experience

## Set Expectations Before the User Begins

Before the user purchases or starts the report, clearly explain that
this is a different experience from a normal Focamai search.

Suggested messaging:

> **Premium Research takes a little longer---by design.**

> Unlike a standard search, Focamai performs a comprehensive research
> process tailored to your needs. It evaluates products, compares expert
> opinions, analyzes long-term ownership, weighs important tradeoffs,
> and prepares a personalized buying report.

> Most reports take **30--60 seconds** to complete. The extra time
> reflects the depth of the research rather than unnecessary waiting.

The waiting time should always be framed as a feature, not a limitation.

The user should think:

> "Great. It's actually doing real work for me."

---not---

> "Why is this taking so long?"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## The Research Ceremony

Do not show a generic loading spinner.

Instead, turn the research process into part of the premium experience.

The user should feel like they have handed the assignment to a
professional researcher.

As real work is completed, display meaningful progress such as:

-   Understanding your priorities...
-   Finding the strongest candidates...
-   Comparing expert opinions...
-   Reviewing owner experiences...
-   Looking for long-term reliability concerns...
-   Identifying meaningful tradeoffs...
-   Separating marketing from substance...
-   Preparing your personalized report...

Whenever possible, each step should correspond to genuine work being
performed.

Occasionally reveal interesting insights without giving away the final
recommendation.

Examples:

> **Interesting finding**

> Multiple reviewers consistently praise Product A's performance but
> mention that replacement filters are unusually expensive.

or

> **Interesting finding**

> Experts largely agree on the top two products, but they disagree
> significantly about which offers better long-term value.

These small glimpses reinforce that the system is actively researching
rather than simply generating text.

When research finishes, conclude with a satisfying transition such as:

> **Research complete.**

> Your personalized buying report is ready.

The report should feel like something that has been carefully prepared
specifically for this user---not something that appeared instantly from
an AI.
